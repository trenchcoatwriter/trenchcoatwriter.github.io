Wilderness

Nine years after the Great War, Don Whitfield finds himself in another fight for survival, this time in the rugged mountains of his native Georgia. Prohibition may have outlawed traditional moonshining, but for Don and his friends, it remains an important means of providing for their families within a dwindling economy. Despite the new dangers, they persist, brewing and selling their wares to a group of northern middlemen who distribute the liquor further north. This changes when their latest bootlegging exchange is disrupted by a group of well-armed revenuers who then pursue them across the mountains in a dangerous cat-and-mouse chase.

Now thrust together, the two disparate groups of renegades must work with each other to evade the feds and reach their homes alive and undiscovered before the harsh winter threatens to end them outright. Their journey crosses more than rough terrain, as the men contemplate the nature of humanity and the scars of war and trauma. For Don, the memories of the no man’s land force him to reexamine his own ideals and the legacy he’ll leave his son. As their ordeal intensifies, Don’s resolve and skills are tested. He knows the route home will be as perilous as the trenches in the war. Especially with a traitor embedded within the group.

The Truth in the LInES

Jonathan Blythe Mythfinder Series

Part of The Calazar Universe©

“What is worse, a total lie, a half-truth, or a bitter fact?” Jon asked. “That’s the thing about mythology, no matter the race or species. In myth, a single story can contain them all.”

At the crossroads of myth and fact, Jonathan Blythe has always had a keen mind for details and an obsession for pursuing answers. But as he is warned upon returning to Calazar from Earth, those who hunt for answers may yet become prey themselves.

In an alternative future, where magic and advanced science coexist alongside a humanity that is one species among millions - both familiar and new - there are still mysteries to be uncovered. Still truth buried behind lost histories, under the lines of sagas and ancient texts, and within the whispers of legends passed along the ages. And some of them are especially dangerous.

When Jon is approached to investigate a series of lost documents pertaining to an ancient kingship feud among the Lycanni race, he swiftly realizes there was more to the story of rival heirs than first glanced. As Jon becomes embroiled in his search, he discovers connections to ancient Earth, conspiracy, and a looming threat both to himself and others.

The more Jon uncovers, the more he contends with a forgotten Lycanni sect, as deadly as any shadow force. On ancient Earth, Lycanni were also called Lycanthropes - Werewolves.

Warlock's Journey

Part of The Calazar Universe©

Book 1: The Shadow in the Rain

Book 2: The Spire in the Woods

Book 3: The Riddle in the Spells

"He heard the voice loudest of all, a forceful presence that drilled into his mind with the precision of a burrowing ant. It overpowered everything. The pounding rain, the screech of metal, the boom of mortars, the cries of war and death. There was only this voice that reverberated against his consciousness with a strength beyond ages, laced with a sting of malice, or of indifference. Then, he saw it. A shadow in the rain..."

On the world of Zeris, Kilian Falstaff leads his mercenary team on what they hope will be a final blow against a treacherous rebel threatening their patron kingdom. Upon infiltrating their enemy’s stronghold though, Kilian uncovers a darker scheme to their enemy’s designs. For this is no mere captured city, but where Vormir Stonehand intends to call down the most potent of magic upon them and all the world.

Facing dire odds, Kilian encounters an entity older than the most ancient of gods and is offered power to save his friends and overcome the threat. The price is his eternal soul.

Now blessed and cursed with this magic, Kilian strives to understand the extent of his bargain and uncover the proprietor of his deal, all while navigating a world that fears the very mention of his kind: Warlock.
